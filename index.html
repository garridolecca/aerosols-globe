About This Visualization

This globe shows a composite view of common atmospheric aerosols including black carbon, dust, and sea salt using near real-time satellite imagery and modeled data.


Aerosols are tiny solid particles and liquid droplets suspended in Earth's atmosphere. They influence climate by scattering and absorbing sunlight and by modifying cloud properties.
