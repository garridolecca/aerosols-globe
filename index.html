About

Real aerosol transport from NASA GEOS-FP (Forward Processing) model. Wind vectors (U10M, V10M) are weighted by each aerosol species' surface mass concentration — particles flow where aerosols actually travel, dense where concentration is high, absent where air is clean.

Data Pipeline

Source: GEOS-FP 0.25° via OPeNDAP (no auth)
Wind: U10M, V10M (10m wind components)
Aerosols: BCSMASS, DUSMASS, SSSMASS, OCSMASS, SO4SMASS
Processing: Wind × normalized(aerosol concentration)
Encoding: U→R, V→G, Magnitude→B in PNG
