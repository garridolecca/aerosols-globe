About This Visualization

Real atmospheric aerosol data from the Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service (CAMS) global forecast model, displayed as Aerosol Optical Depth (AOD) at 550nm.


Each layer shows a distinct aerosol species: total composite, biomass burning (black carbon), mineral dust, and sea salt. Wind flow particles show atmospheric circulation from GFS reanalysis.
